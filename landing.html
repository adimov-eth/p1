Frequently asked questions

What's included in the membership?

Everything: green fee, caddy, and golf cart at all 80+ partner courses. No additional costs at check-out (except guest fees and F&B).

How many rounds do we get?

144 rounds annually for standard members, 156 rounds for Founders' Circle members.

How far ahead can we book?

Varies by course. Most courses offer 7-14 day booking windows. Your concierge will advise on specific venue policies.

Can we invite guests?

Yes. Up to 3 guests per user per round. Guest fees apply: ฿2,800-4,500 depending on course tier (all-inclusive for guests too).

What's the cancellation policy?
