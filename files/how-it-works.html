Policies

Our Bangkok concierge responds within 2 hours during business hours (7 days a week). If your preferred slot is unavailable, we'll offer alternatives at similar courses within your booking window. All confirmations include course details, tee time, and any special requirements.
Courses publish blackout dates 60 days in advance for tournaments and major events. Blackout dates vary by course. We maintain a shared calendar accessible to all members. Contact the concierge to check availability during high-demand periods.
Seat holders can invite guests on weekdays without restrictions. Weekend guesting is limited to maintain member access. Each seat holder receives 2 guaranteed weekend slots per month which can be used for guesting. Guest green fees are covered under the same ceiling structure.
Companies can reassign seats twice per year. Contact the concierge at least 7 days before the effective date. The new seat holder receives access credentials and inherits any unused credits and weekend allocations. All existing bookings transfer to the new holder.
