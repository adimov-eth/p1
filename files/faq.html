Frequently asked questions

If it's not here, ask the concierge.

Membership

The 700,000 THB annual fee includes 2 named seats, 150 shared round credits, green fee coverage up to posted ceilings, concierge booking service with 2-hour response time, guaranteed weekend allocations, and annual usage statements. Cart and caddie fees are paid separately at each course.
Currently, Prime Corporate is a 2-seat membership only. We're developing options for companies requiring additional seats. Contact the concierge to join the waitlist for multi-seat plans.
Unused credits do not carry over to the next membership year. Credits reset on your annual renewal date. We recommend tracking your remaining credits through regular usage statements or by contacting the concierge.

Courses

Billing

Admin

You receive an annual usage statement showing all bookings, credits used, and remaining balance. Ad-hoc statements are available upon request from the concierge. This helps finance teams track entertainment expenses and plan for the following year.

Operations

Each course has its own booking window, typically 7-21 days. Weekday slots are easier to secure with shorter notice. Weekend slots should be booked as early as possible within the booking window. The concierge will advise on optimal booking timing for each course.
Cancellations with 48+ hours notice incur no penalty. No-shows or cancellations within 48 hours deduct 0.5 credit. Contact the concierge immediately if you need to cancel. Course-specific cancellation policies may apply for premium venues.
